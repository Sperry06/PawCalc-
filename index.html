How we calculate these figures

Purchase prices are based on KC registration data, rescue centre fee averages, and breeder listings across the UK (2025–2026). Location multipliers reflect regional cost-of-living differences. 🏥 Always insure from day one — puppy illnesses can cost thousands.

💊 Generic flea/worming treatments work as well as branded at half the price.
🐾 Rescue dogs have lower purchase costs and are often already vaccinated.
📅 Annual pet dental checks prevent expensive surgery later.
