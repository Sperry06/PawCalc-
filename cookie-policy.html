COOKIE POLICY

Last updated June 13, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and (where you have consented) deliver relevant advertising. Under UK PECR (Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations) and UK GDPR, we are required to tell you what cookies we use and to obtain your consent before placing any non-essential cookies.

3. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Strictly necessary cookies enable core functions of this website such as security and your consent record. These cookies do not require your consent under PECR; however, you can block them in your browser settings, which may affect how the site functions.

4. Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use PawCalc — for example, which calculators are most popular. This data is aggregated and anonymised. Analytics cookies are only placed after you give consent.

5. Advertising Cookies

PawCalc displays contextual advertisements. If you have given consent, advertising partners may set cookies to measure ad performance and serve relevant ads. You can opt out at any time via the Your Online Choices portal or the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out.

6. Your Choices

You can control cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings — All modern browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. See your browser's help pages for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out — Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Withdraw consent — You can withdraw your consent to analytics cookies at any time by clearing your cookies and declining when next asked.

7. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on PawCalc are set by third-party services (e.g. Google Analytics, advertising partners). We do not control these cookies. Please review the relevant third-party privacy and cookie policies for further information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page periodically to stay informed.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at pawcalcc@gmail.com.
