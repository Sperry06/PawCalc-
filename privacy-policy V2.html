1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

In Short: We collect personal information that you choose to provide to us via the email sign-up form.

PawCalc does not require registration or account creation. We collect personal information only when you voluntarily submit the optional email sign-up form on our website.

Personal Information Provided by You. The personal information we collect via the sign-up form may include: your email address; your town or city (optional); and, on the dog breed calculator, the selected dog breed (optional).

Sensitive Information. We do not process sensitive information.

Information automatically collected

In Short: Some information — such as your IP address and browser characteristics — is collected automatically when you visit our Services, but only where you have given cookie consent.

Where you have given consent to analytics cookies, Google Analytics 4 automatically collects certain technical information when you visit the site. This may include your IP address (anonymised by Google before storage), browser type, device type, operating system, language preferences, and referring URL. Calculator inputs are not transmitted to any third party — all calculations run locally in your browser. We do not use precise device geolocation; we only collect the town or city you optionally self-enter in the sign-up form.

We do not collect personal information from any third-party sources (such as public databases, marketing partners, or data brokers).
